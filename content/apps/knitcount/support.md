## Frequently Asked Questions

### How do I add a new project?

Tap the **+** button in the top-right corner of the main screen. Enter a project name and tap **Add**. Two default counters ("Main Rows" and "Pattern Repeats") are created automatically so you can start counting right away.

### How do I add more counters to a project?

Open your project, then tap **Add Counter** at the bottom of the screen. Give it a name and set an optional step size.

### What is the step size?

The step size controls how much the counter changes with each tap. A step of 1 (default) increases or decreases by 1. Set it to 2 to count every other row, or any number that matches your pattern.

### How do I undo a mis-tap?

An **Undo** bar appears at the bottom of the screen immediately after each count change. Tap it within 5 seconds to reverse the last action. This works for both increment and decrement.

### Why does the screen stay on while I'm counting?

KnitCount keeps the screen awake by default so you don't have to wake your phone mid-row. You can toggle this with the lock icon in the toolbar — tap it to allow normal screen timeout.

### How do I rename or reset a counter?

Long-press any counter card to open the edit sheet. You can rename the counter, change its step size, or reset its value to 0.

### Will my counts be lost if I close the app?

No. KnitCount saves your counts automatically after every tap. Close the app, take a call, put your phone down — everything will be exactly as you left it when you return.

### How do I delete a project?

On the main project list, swipe left on any project to reveal a **Delete** button. This will also delete all counters belonging to that project.

### How do I request a refund?

Refunds are handled by Apple at [reportaproblem.apple.com](https://reportaproblem.apple.com).
