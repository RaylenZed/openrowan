## Frequently Asked Questions

### How do I calculate tiles for an irregular room?

Break the room into rectangular sections, calculate each section separately, then add the totals together. TileCalc's project feature lets you add multiple areas to a single project.

### What waste factor should I use?

- **Straight lay (grid):** 10% waste
- **Diagonal lay (45°):** 15% waste
- **Complex patterns or cuts around cabinets:** 15–20% waste

TileCalc sets a default of 10%. Increase it for diagonal patterns or rooms with many cuts.

### The tile size I need isn't listed.

Use the custom size option — enter any width and height in mm, cm, or inches.

### How do I calculate grout?

Grout estimates are based on tile size, joint width, and area. Enter your joint width in the project settings and TileCalc will include a grout quantity estimate.

### Can I export my project results?

You can share a summary using the share button on the results screen. This exports a plain-text summary of your project dimensions, tile size, and material quantities.

### How do I request a refund?

Refunds are handled by Apple at [reportaproblem.apple.com](https://reportaproblem.apple.com).
