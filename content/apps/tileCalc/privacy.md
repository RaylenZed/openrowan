## Overview

TileCalc ("the App") is developed by OpenRowan Studio. This is a simple, offline calculator. This page exists to be transparent about what we do — and don't — collect.

**Short version:** TileCalc collects essentially no data. All calculations happen on your device. We don't know who you are, what rooms you're tiling, or how you use the App.

## Data We Collect

### Crash Reports

We collect anonymous crash reports to help us fix bugs. These include your device model, iOS version, and a crash stack trace. They contain no personally identifiable information.

## What We Do NOT Collect

- We do not collect your name, location, or contact information
- We do not track your usage or behavior
- We do not use advertising networks or analytics SDKs
- We do not send any calculation data off your device
- We do not require an account or login

## Data Storage

Your saved projects and calculations are stored locally on your device only. We have no server and no database.

## Children's Privacy

This app is not directed at children under 13.

We will post any changes here with an updated date.
