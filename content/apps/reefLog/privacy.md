## Overview

ReefLog ("the App") is developed by Leilei Zhang. This is a simple, offline reef aquarium tracker. This page exists to be transparent about what we do — and don't — collect.

**Short version:** ReefLog collects essentially no data. All your tank records, parameter logs, and coral inventory are stored only on your device. We have no server and no database.

## Data We Collect

### Crash Reports

We collect anonymous crash reports to help us fix bugs. These include your device model, iOS version, and a crash stack trace. They contain no personally identifiable information.

## What We Do NOT Collect

- We do not collect your name, location, or contact information
- We do not track your usage or behavior
- We do not use advertising networks or analytics SDKs
- We do not send any tank data, parameter readings, or coral records off your device
- We do not require an account or login

## Data Storage

All data you enter in ReefLog — tank profiles, water parameter readings, maintenance logs, and coral inventory — is stored locally on your device only using Apple's SwiftData framework. Nothing is synced to any server.

## iCloud

ReefLog does not use iCloud sync. Your data remains on your device only and is not backed up to iCloud.

## Children's Privacy

This app is not directed at children under 13.
