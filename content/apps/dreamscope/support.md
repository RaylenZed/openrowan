## Frequently Asked Questions

### How do I log a dream?

Tap the **+** button on the Journal tab or the "Log today's dream" card on the Home screen. Enter a title, describe your dream, choose a mood and sleep quality, and optionally add tags. Tap **Analyze with AI** to save.

### What does AI analysis do?

When you save a dream, the App sends the title and description to an AI service to generate a short interpretation. Over time, Dreamscope also identifies recurring themes and symbols across your entries.

### How do I generate dream artwork?

Enable **Generate Dream Art** when logging a new dream (Pro subscribers only). The AI will create a surreal image based on your dream description. Artwork appears in the Journal and Insights gallery.

### How do I cancel my subscription?

Subscriptions are managed by Apple, not within the App. To cancel:
1. Open **Settings** on your iPhone
2. Tap your name → **Subscriptions**
3. Find **Dreamscope** and tap **Cancel Subscription**

Alternatively, visit [reportaproblem.apple.com](https://reportaproblem.apple.com).

### I was charged but the App shows no active subscription.

Force-quit and reopen the App. If it still shows inactive, go to **Profile → Restore Purchases**. If the issue persists, email us with your Apple ID region (not your Apple ID itself).

### How do I delete my account?

Go to **Profile** tab → scroll to the bottom → tap **Delete Account**. This permanently deletes your account and all dream entries. This action cannot be undone.

### Where is my data stored?

Your dreams are stored on Cloudflare's infrastructure to enable cloud sync. See our [Privacy Policy](/apps/dreamscope/privacy/) for full details.

### How do I request a refund?

Refunds are managed by Apple at [reportaproblem.apple.com](https://reportaproblem.apple.com).
