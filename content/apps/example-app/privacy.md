## Overview

Example App ("the App") is developed by OpenWillow Studio ("we", "us", "our"). This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data.

**Short version:** We collect as little data as possible. We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

## Information We Collect

### Crash Reports

We collect anonymous crash reports to help us identify and fix bugs. These reports include device type, iOS version, and a stack trace of the crash. They do not include any personally identifiable information.

### Information We Do NOT Collect

- We do not collect your name, email, or contact information
- We do not track your location
- We do not access your camera, microphone, or contacts
- We do not use advertising SDKs or analytics platforms
- We do not create user profiles or track behavior across apps

## Data Storage

All app data is stored locally on your device. We do not operate servers that store your personal data.

## Third-Party Services

This app does not integrate any third-party SDKs that collect data.

## Children's Privacy

This app is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The updated version will be posted at this URL with a revised "Last updated" date.
