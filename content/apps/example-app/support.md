## Get Help

We're happy to help! If you're having trouble with Example App, here are the best ways to reach us.

## Contact Support

We typically respond within 1–2 business days.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### The app crashed. What should I do?

Please force-quit the app and reopen it. If the problem persists, delete and reinstall the app. If it still crashes, please email us with a description of what you were doing when it crashed and your device model and iOS version.

### How do I delete my data?

All your data is stored locally on your device. To delete it, simply delete the app from your iPhone. Your data will be permanently removed.

### I have a feature request. Where do I send it?

We'd love to hear your ideas! Email us at [ray@raylenzed.com](mailto:ray@raylenzed.com) with the subject line "Feature Request."

### I found a bug. How do I report it?

Please email [ray@raylenzed.com](mailto:ray@raylenzed.com) with:
- A description of the bug
- Steps to reproduce it
- Your device model and iOS version
